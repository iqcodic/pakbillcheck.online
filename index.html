FESCO - Faisalabad Electric Supply Company

Learn about Pakistan's major electricity distribution company serving millions of customers

About FESCO

Faisalabad Electric Supply Company (FESCO) is one of Pakistan's largest electricity distribution companies, serving over 4 million customers across central Punjab province.

Established to provide reliable electricity distribution services, FESCO covers major industrial and agricultural areas, contributing significantly to Pakistan's economic development.

Help Center - Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about FESCO bills and our services

How do I check my FESCO bill online?

Simply enter your bill reference number in the bill checker form on our homepage. You'll be redirected to the official FESCO portal to view your complete bill details.

Where can I find my bill reference number?

Your bill reference number is printed on your physical electricity bill, usually at the top or in a prominent location. It's typically a 14-digit number.

Can I pay my bill through this website?

We provide bill checking services and redirect you to the official FESCO website where you can make payments securely through various methods.

Is my information secure?

Yes, we prioritize your privacy and security. We don't store your bill information and redirect you directly to official FESCO services.

What if I can't find my bill reference number?

If you've lost your physical bill, you can contact FESCO customer service at 1991 or visit your nearest FESCO office with your CNIC to get bill details.

How often are bills generated?

FESCO generates electricity bills monthly. You should receive your bill within the first week of each month for the previous month's consumption.
